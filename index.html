About

Engineering background

Engineering experience across mechanical design, mechatronics, soft robotics, wearable hardware, and human-subject biomechanics for systems that support movement, rehabilitation, and prosthetic function.

I work across the full development cycle: problem definition, CAD design, prototyping, actuation and sensing, finite element analysis, experimental setup, data collection, and performance evaluation.

This portfolio highlights projects in transradial prosthesis emulation, cerebral palsy gait training, portable pneumatic systems, inflatable actuator composites, soft insole sensors, fabric-based soft ankle modules, and automated security gate design.
